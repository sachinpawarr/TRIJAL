How It's Made

The TRIJAL Process

No shortcuts, no chemicals. Just patience and tradition.

1

Seed Sourcing

We source only premium, non-GMO seeds directly from local farming communities.

2

Sun Drying

Seeds are naturally sun-dried to optimal moisture levels to concentrate flavor and nutrients.

3

Wood Pressing

Crushed in a traditional wooden 'ghani' at low RPM to ensure the temperature never rises, keeping nutrients intact.

4

Natural Settling & Filtering

The oil is allowed to settle naturally and filtered through cotton cloth without any chemical solvents.

5

Bottling

Packaged in eco-friendly glass bottles to preserve the pristine quality, taste, and aroma.
